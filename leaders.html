Mike Nahoum

CFO and VP Operations

Prior to Mike becoming the acting Financial Director* of Continent 8 Technologies PLC in April 2006 Mike was the CFO and VP Operations of Mohawk internet Technologies from September 2001. Prior to that Mike served as Chief Financial Officer of Interaction Restaurants Ltd for 5 years. During that time, the company was ranked among the top 50 Canadian-based restaurant companies in terms of sales. Prior to that, he was Chief Financial Officer of The Marine Group, a real estate development company. Mike is a Chartered Accountant and articled at Zittrer Siblin Stein Levine (which later joined Ernst & Young) until he left as a Senior Manager. Mike has a Bachelor of Commerce from Concordia University and is a graduate of McGill University.

*Financial directorship and consulting services will be provided to the Company by a Canadian company wholly owned and controlled by Mike Nahoum.

John Stone

Chief Technology Officer

John joined Continent 8 in 2010 from Cisco Systems where he was their CTO for Ireland, he previously also worked for Cara, a subsidiary of Aer Lingus.

As CTO for Continent 8, John is responsible for translating technology innovation into business value for Continent 8 and its eGaming customers. John is responsible for the strategic direction of internal infrastructure and services to support Continent 8’s extensive global network, multi-jurisdictional eGaming platform and support organization. In addition, John is also responsible for the definition, development and delivery of current and new services to address customer requirements in this growing and challenging marketplace.

John holds a degree from Dublin City University along with three Diploma’s in Information Technology from DCU, Electrical Engineering from DIT Kevin Street, and Business & Finance from the IMI.

Nick Nally

Chief Development Officer

Nick has 20 years experience in many sectors of the IT & telecommunications industry encompassing senior management, consultancy, sales, carrier relations, engineering and business development.

Most recently, Nick set up and developed Packet Exchange’s business operations in Ireland. Nick is also a consultant to the Department of Communications in Ireland and has worked on a number of initiatives including the Irish Connectivity Project and related data centre activity which was a key part of the Irish government’s economic development program. Prior to this Nick held a number of positions in Ireland and the US including Sales Director for WorldPort, a managed hosting provider; Head of Wholesale & Carrier Services for WorldCom in Ireland where he set up the wholesale operation; General Manager for MCI WorldCom in Ireland and Vice President of Business Development with Eircom in the US. Nick holds Electronic Engineering and Master of Industrial Engineering degrees from University College, Dublin.
