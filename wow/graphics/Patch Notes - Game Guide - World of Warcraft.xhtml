The Iron Tide

  • Overview
  • Patch 6.0.2
Contents
Treacherous DevelopmentsInfiltrate Blackrock SpireSkirmishesNew Character Models

With the release of Patch 6.0.2, a number of features and changes that aren't directly tied to Warlords of Draenor have been added to World of Warcraft, including revisions to the talent system and the abilities of every class in the game. We've also added several brand-new features: Skirmishes; cross-realm functionality for Group Finder; a new pre-expansion event; and even more!

 

Treacherous Developments

Hundreds of strange-looking orcs are violently pouring into Azeroth through the Dark Portal in the Blasted Lands, killing everything that stands in their path. Nethergarde and Okril’lon have already fallen. The Iron Horde invasion has begun.

Word of this surprise attack sends players rushing to the Blasted Lands to reckon with the barbarous infiltration. It’s apparent to both the Horde and the Alliance that this is only the first salvo in a much larger conflict, and the only way to win is to take the fight directly to the Iron Horde . . .

 
 

Infiltrate Blackrock Spire

Available to level-90 characters for a limited time, this specially revamped five-player version of Upper Blackrock Spire sets the stage for the coming counterstrike against the Iron Horde. Here, you will face Orebender Gor'ashan, Kyrak, and Commander Tharbek as you begin to take the fight to the enemy and sharpen the skills (and swords) you’ll need to succeed on an unwelcoming alien world.

 

Skirmishes

Though Arena Season 15 has come to an end, Arena enthusiasts can now enjoy unranked Skirmishes. You can solo queue or form your own group for a 2v2 or 3v3 match and hone your skills for next season!

 

New Character Models

All the Classic races have been updated with brand new character models. You can now also visit the barber shop in Stormwind and Orgrimmar to tweak your character's facial appearance!
